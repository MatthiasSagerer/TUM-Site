  • In January 2023 I finished my Bachelor's Thesis at Prof. Dr. Andreas Bausch's Chair for Cellular Biophysics (Bauschlab, Center for functional protein folding): "Optimization of Machine Learning Growth Prediction for Cell Culture Experiments"
    The growth prediction for pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma cells (PDAC; pancreatic cancer) was optimized. A progressive growing generative adversarial network (PGGAN) was utilized for future frame prediction on microscopy data. Possible use cases include the use of predictions as digital controls during the testing of active agents for cancer treatment in the research stage.
