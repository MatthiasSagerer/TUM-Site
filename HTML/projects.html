  • Programming-wise I'm currently mostly working on my bachelor thesis in the research group of Prof. Dr. Andreas Bausch (Bauschlab) at the Center for functional protein folding.
    The goal is to develop an automated way to use an AI powered future frame prediction algorithm for biological cell culture experiments. Specifically I'm predicting the growth of two kinds of cancer cells (also as 3D organoids). This prediction can then be compared to the real cells (organoids) which have been treated with an active agent in the research stage.
    Once its finished, corrected and defended, the thesis will be available to download.
